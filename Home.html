In the 21st century, video games are no longer the "time killers" or "digital drugs", in the opinion of the last several generations. They have become a part of the colorful cultures that composed the civilization of human beings. However, both the variation of video games and the public's acceptance happened gradually. It was an evolution of video games. It is processed simultaneously around the world but affected by different cultures in different countries. In China, the development of such a culture was extremely difficult due to the old's conventions and the less democratic environment. However, with the development of globalization, evolution is happening.
